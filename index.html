About Me

My name is Bill, and I am a dedicated second-year student at FPT University, where I currently hold a 30% full scholarship based on my excellent entrance exam results. With 1.5 years of experience as an instructor, I have been teaching Scratch, Python, Lego Wedo, HTML/CSS, and other academic subjects not only to elementary students but also to children aged 6 to 18 who have experience in coding and communicating in English.

My ultimate career goal is to become a Game Developer, and my strong passion for game development started when I was in 9th grade. Despite being judged as someone who only wasted time and lacked practicality, I remained committed to my chosen path, graduating from EMASI International Bilingual High School with a remarkable academic record. I was recognized as one of the top 5 outstanding graduates of 2022 and received an academic award as one of the 5 most outstanding graduates of the school. Additionally, I was admitted directly to the first year of FPT University, thanks to my IELTS 5, and received a 30% scholarship.

During my high school graduation period, I applied to BrightChamps and DigiUni and chose DigiUni as my first workplace. There, I was quickly promoted to an instructor within one month of onboarding in July, achieving several accomplishments. Check my teacher profile here I am currently working as a Computer Science Study Advisor at Marathon Education since March. Additionally, I have previously worked as a Game Developer Intern at Golden Sea Studio. I am excited to have had the opportunity to contribute to both organizations in the field of computer science education and game development

Given my track record of success, I am confident that my skills and abilities have been demonstrated through my achievements, and I am now eager to take on new challenges and excel in a new position.
